# Rhythm Blocks

A rhythm game where notes scroll across a grid and the player taps tiles in time to the beat.

## Controls

- **Keyboard:**
    - `S`, `D`, `F` for Left 3 Lanes
    - `J`, `K`, `L` for Right 3 Lanes
- **Touch / Mouse:**
    - Tap or click on the column lanes at the bottom of the screen.

## Gameplay

- Hit notes as they cross the Hit Line.
- Perfect hits give more points and build combo.
- Maintain combo for score multiplier.
- Patterns (multiple simultaneous notes) give bonus points.

## Features

- Procedurally generated daily tracks.
- Authored charts.
- Combo system.
- Audio-first timing engine.
