# Blackjack

A classic casino card game with arcade pacing.

## Rules
- **Objective**: Beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.
- **Card Values**: Face cards are 10, Aces are 1 or 11.
- **Dealer**: Stands on Soft 17.
- **Blackjack**: Pays 3:2.

## Controls
- **Hit**: Take another card.
- **Stand**: End your turn.
- **Double**: Double your bet and take exactly one card.

## Scoring
Score is based on your final chip count.
 Daily challenges use a seeded deck for fair competition.
